RULES & INFO
Game Rules
• Players need to be registered on the website before enrolling in any kind of tournament.

• Players need to join the server 15 minutes before the match starts. Warmup time will be allowed before the start of the match.

• The details for joining the server will be shared 30 minutes before the scheduled start time of the match in My Tournaments section.

• Starting side will be chosen by an additional knife round before the start of the match.

• The first team to win 16 rounds will be declared victor of the match and will advance in the tournament.

• No outside applications / executables / dll’s / workshop items, read any method of gaining an advantage over the other team is allowed in the tournament.

• Players not ready within 15 minutes (outside technical issues handled by an Admin) of their match will forfeit that match.

How To Play
• Open the My Tournaments Section and look for the game server’s ip and password.

• Open Counter-Strike:Global Offensive and go to play section.

• On the drop down list select the option for “Browse Community Servers”.

• Search for “Helixios_eSports” in the search bar and select it.

• When prompted, enter the password which was given on the tournament section along with other info.

• Your game will start connecting to the server.
